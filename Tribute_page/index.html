TIMELINE
October 15, 1931
Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu, India, to Jainulabdeen Marakayar and Ashiamma.
1954
Graduates in physics from St. Joseph's College, Tiruchirappalli. He moves to Madras to pursue further education after graduation.
1955
Joins the Madras Institute of Technology (MIT) for a degree in aeronautical engineering.
1960
Completes his degree and joins the Aeronautical Development Establishment of the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) as a scientist.
1969
Transfers to the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) and becomes the project director of India's first indigenous Satellite Launch Vehicle (SLV-III) program.
1980
Kalam leads Indiaâ€™s efforts to enter the space club by putting the Rohini satellite in Earth's orbit with the first indigenous (SLV-III)
1980-1990
As the chief of the Integrated Guided Missile Development Programme, he is credited with the development and operationalisation of Agni and Prithvi missiles. For this he was also known as 'Missile Man of India'
1981
The Government of India honoured him with the Padma Bhushan for his contribution to Indian space research.
1990
Receives the Padma Vibhushan award from the Government of India for his contribution to India's defence program.
1992 - 1999
Kalam served as the Chief Scientific Adviser to the Prime Minister and Secretary of the Defence Research and Development Organisation
1997
Kalam received India's highest civilian honour, the Bharat Ratna, for his contribution to the scientific research and modernisation of defence technology in India.Kalam awarded with Indira Gandhi Award for National Integration in the same year.
1998
India successfully conducts nuclear tests under his leadership as the Chief Project Coordinator, Pokhran-II nuclear tests.
2002 - 2007
Kalam served as the 11th president of India, succeeding K. R. Narayanan.
2012
Kalam launched a programme for the youth of India called the What Can I Give Movement, with a central theme of defeating corruption.
July 27, 2015
Abdul Kalam died of a cardiac arrest while giving a lecture at the IIM Shillong.
